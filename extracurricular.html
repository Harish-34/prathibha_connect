Extracurricular Activities at Prathibha Blooming High School

At Prathibha Blooming High School, we believe in the holistic development of our students through a variety of extracurricular activities. Below are some of the key activities available:

Sports

We offer various sports activities, including basketball, football, cricket, and athletics. Students are encouraged to participate in inter-school competitions.

Arts and Crafts

Students can explore their creativity through arts and crafts workshops, including painting, pottery, and sculpture making.

Music and Dance

Our music and dance programs help students develop their artistic talents, with options for choir, band, and various dance forms.

Debate and Public Speaking

Students can enhance their communication skills and critical thinking through our debate club and public speaking events.

Drama and Theatre

Our drama club provides students with opportunities to participate in school plays, enhancing their confidence and teamwork skills.

Environmental Club

Students can join the environmental club to engage in activities focused on sustainability and conservation efforts within the school and community.
