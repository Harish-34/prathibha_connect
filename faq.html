Frequently Asked Questions

What is the school's academic curriculum?

Our academic curriculum follows a comprehensive framework designed to meet the needs of all students. We offer a blend of core subjects, electives, and extracurricular activities to ensure a well-rounded education.

How can I enroll my child in Prathibha Blooming High School?

To enroll your child, please visit our Admissions page for detailed information on the application process and requirements.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, arts, and various clubs to foster student interests and talents.

What are the school hours?

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Specific timings may vary based on the grade level.

Is transportation provided for students?

Yes, we provide school transportation services. Please contact the administration office for more details on routes and schedules.
